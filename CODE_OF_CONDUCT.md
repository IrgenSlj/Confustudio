# Code of Conduct

CONFUstudio aims to be a welcoming, productive, and respectful community.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and constructive in all interactions.
- Assume good intent. Ask questions before assigning blame.
- Give thoughtful feedback on code, not on people.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory remarks.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or pull requests.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behavior that goes against this code, contact the project maintainer by opening a private issue or emailing the maintainer at the address listed in `package.json`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, edit, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in a ban from the project.
